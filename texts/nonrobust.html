Geostatistic interpolation is based on character of spatial value. This character can be described by variograms. The basic variogram is called experimental.

Formula of experimental variogram:

The experimental variogram is made of isolated points, it is necessary to aproximate it by the theoretical variogram. Model of theoretical variogram is choosen by eye according to experimental variogram. The most important models are exponential, spheric and gauss.

There are formulas of each variogram. Constant C means sill. Sill is value from which the others dont change. Constant a means range, it is distance where the function gets its maximum. γ (h) is semivariance of variable z for distance h.
